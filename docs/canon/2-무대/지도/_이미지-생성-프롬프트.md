# 🎨 지도 이미지 생성 프롬프트 팩

> 사용법: 아래 영문 프롬프트를 이미지 생성기에 붙여넣기. 미드저니는 끝에 `--ar 3:2 --v 6` 권장.
> 생성 후 **캐논 체크리스트**와 대조 — 하나라도 어긋나면 재생성.

## 1. 아스트라리스 세계 전도

```
Hand-drawn fantasy world map on aged parchment, antique cartography style, sepia ink with muted
green/red/blue/grey washes. FIVE continents on one ocean: a glacial continent "FROST" in the far
north; a shadowy twilight continent "OBELISK" isolated in the far southeast open sea, drawn half-faded
as if maps refuse to record it, dotted with black monoliths; a lush forest continent "ETHER" in the
center-west, covered by giant world-tree roots; a red desert wasteland "CRIMSON" southeast of ETHER with
a scorched imperial capital; and a scattered archipelago "OCEANIC" in the center-east acting as the
trade hub of all sea lanes. Dashed golden trade routes between continents, a narrow strait choke
point marked with chains, an ice-breaker sea lane to the north. A broken compass rose (one arm
snapped off) in the corner. Korean fantasy webnovel atlas, elegant serif labels, no text gibberish,
high detail, top-down map view.
```
캐논 체크: 대륙 5개 / 프로스트=북서 / 에테르=중서 최대 / 크림슨=에테르 남동 / 오벨리스크=최남동 원해·반쯤 지워진 표현 / 해양=동측 군도 허브 / 나침반은 부러진 모양(작품 모티프).

## 2. 에테르 대륙 전도

```
Fantasy continent map, antique atlas style on parchment, top-down. Continent "ETHER — the Living
Forest": northern mountain range with a deep silver forest sanctuary (elven spirit union capital
hidden in its heart); CENTER is a holy basin plateau — a theocratic Saint Nation with a white
cathedral capital on the highest hill, a volcanic ruin and a ceremonial stair nearby, ringed by a
dashed border; a FLOATING hexagonal mage city hovering 3km above the center with eight colored
towers, casting a soft shadow; the mid-west is a feudal kingdoms alliance (crown capital, shield
fortress in NW mountains, a 300km mountain defense line on the west frontier); golden granary
plains in the east; lake-and-hill country in the south with port cities on the coast; free trade
city-states scattered at every crossroads, two twin hub cities at the exact center with four great
highways radiating out. A canyon bridge city in the north, a casino city in a western canyon, and a
small walled appraiser town near the theocracy's border marked with a dotted circle and a question
mark. Muted greens and golds, serif labels, cartographic symbols, no gibberish text.
```
캐논 체크: 성국=중앙 분지(백색 대성당 수도) / 부유 마법도시=중앙 상공 / 왕국연합=중서부 / 은성 숲=북부 / 곡창=동부 / 골든마르크·크로스로드=최중앙 4대 간선 / 베르나=국경 인근 점선 물음표.

## 3. 베르나 시가도

```
Bird's-eye view illustrated town map of a small walled market town, fantasy webnovel style, warm
lantern-lit evening. Dense low rooftops connected eave-to-eave (a rooftop runner's town), a central
bazaar street with stalls (spice grandmother, old map stall, wax doll carver), an appraiser's alley
just off the market entrance with a tiny appraisal shop whose sign reads only a plain plaque, the
shop back-to-back with a map seller across a narrow alley. A shadow black market deeper in the
alleys with low-hung lanterns, a warehouse lane behind the market, a small stream with one stone
bridge at the edge of the backstreets, a single town gate onto a highway. Beyond the walls: open
fields, a low ridge line, and a border fortress town on the horizon. No harbor, no river port, no
noble quarter. Sepia and amber palette, hand-drawn, cozy but noir.
```
캐논 체크(원고): 지붕길 저층 밀집 / 시장 어귀→감정사 거리→감정소(지도 가게와 등 맞댐) / 물은 개천+다리 1 / 항구·귀족구 없음 / 성벽 밖 들판·능선·국경.
